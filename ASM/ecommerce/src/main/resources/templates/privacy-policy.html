Privacy Policy

Blue Store is committed to protecting customers' privacy and personal information.

1. Information Collection

We collect customer information to facilitate shopping and improve our services, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Payment information

2. Use of Information

Customer information is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing orders and delivering products.
  • Providing customer support and product information.
  • Enhancing the shopping experience.
  • Sending promotional notifications (with customer consent).

3. Information Security

Blue Store is committed to safeguarding customers' personal information with strict security measures. We do not share information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Compliance with legal requirements.
  • Providing necessary information to shipping partners for delivery.

4. Customer Rights

Customers have the right to:

  • Request corrections or deletion of personal information.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional messages.
  • Contact us for inquiries regarding data privacy.
