Warranty Policy

1. Warranty Conditions

  • The product is still within the warranty period.
  • The defect is caused by the manufacturer (not including natural wear and tear, damage due to impact, or external factors).
  • The product retains its warranty seal, has not been opened or repaired by a third party.

2. Warranty Period

The warranty period varies depending on the product type, ranging from 6 months to 24 months.

Specific warranty information is provided on the invoice or warranty card.

3. Warranty Methods

  • Repair Warranty: If the product is defective, Blue Store will repair it free of charge.
  • Replacement Warranty: If the product cannot be repaired, customers may receive a replacement of the same or equivalent value.

4. Cases Not Covered by Warranty

  • Damage caused by external factors such as drops, impacts, or water exposure.
  • Explosions or fires due to improper use or unstable power sources.
  • Warranty seals are torn, altered, or the product has been repaired by unauthorized parties.

5. Warranty Process

Customers can bring the product to a Blue Store location or send it by mail along with the purchase invoice.

The average processing time for warranty service is 5 - 15 business days, depending on the issue.
